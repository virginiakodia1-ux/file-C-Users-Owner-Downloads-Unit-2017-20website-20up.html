About us

Our plots are easy to reach, the paths are wide, and there’s plenty of seating if you fancy a breather. We grow seasonal vegetables, herbs, and fruit, and we keep a corner for wildlife. You’re welcome whether you’re a complete beginner or an old hand.

  • Weekly meet-up: Wednesdays, 10:00–12:00
  • Where: Riverside Allotments, Plot 14–28
  • Facilities: accessible loo, tool shed, tea corner

News & tips

Autumn
